clause Clause 9
title CONTRIBUTION IN RESPECT OF COMPENSATION

Amendment proposed [this day], No. 127, in page 11, line 7, after 'indemnity', insert—

"(5) For the avoidance of doubt it is hereby declared that an act done by a trade union or other person for the purpose of persuading an employer to agree to pay a sum of money in consequence of an order under subsection (2) above:

  1. shall not by virtue only of this section be regarded as an unlawful act or the use of unlawful means for the purpose of establishing liability in tort; and
  2. shall be regarded as connected with the employment or non-employment of the person who made the original complaint against the employer within the meaning of section 29 of the 1974 Act;
