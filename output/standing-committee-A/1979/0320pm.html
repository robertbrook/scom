What kind of civil wrong? Not every civil wrong; only the very special kind which is specified in section 13 of the Trade Union and Labour Relations Act 1974

"to induce another person to break a contract"

"It shall be lawful for one or more persons in contemplation or furtherance of a trade dispute to attend at or near … a place where another person works or carries on business … for the purpose only of peacefully obtaining or communicating information"—

first leg—
