An administration order is a method of bringing together all the assets of a debtor so that, instead of each creditor taking a separate peck at the car case, we can organise the debts, make sure that everyone gets a fair deal and organise the assets so that they are available to pay other debts. That is the purpose of an administration order, and it is intended to do it in such a way that one is spared the more complicated, more severe and more expensive machinery of bankruptcy. which might be appropriate when one is dealing with much bigger debts and assets.
