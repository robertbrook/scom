 It is to try to improve this point that I have submitted my Amendment.

"The general rule that meetings on highways are trespasses is derived from private law. It gives no weight to the public interest in freedom of expression and the time is ripe for reconsideration by the courts."

 I believe that this very narrow legal definition of the right of assembly is one which is kept in the cupboard and trotted out from time to time to suit a particular occasion.

"Everyone has the right to freedom of assembly and freedom of association".
