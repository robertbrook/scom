debates

Prisons (Alcohol Testing) Bill

10.30 am

clause Clause 1
title POWER TO TEST PRISONERS FOR ALCOHOL

Question proposed, That the clause stand part of the Bill.

Mr. Bernard Jenkin (Colchester, North): I thank the Committee for supporting my presentation of the Bill. The purpose of the Bill was outlined extensively on Col. 4 Second Reading in December and I am most grateful to my hon. Friend the Member for Poole (Mr. Ward) for giving me the opportunity to present it in Committee. I do not think that it is necessary to add anything further to our discussions in December.

Question put and agreed to.

Clause 1 ordered to stand part of the Bill.

Clauses 2 and 3 ordered to stand part of the Bill.

Bill to be reported, without amendment.

Committee rose at twenty-nine minutes to Eleven o'clock.
