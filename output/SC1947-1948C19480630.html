The effect of this Amendment is to make the agency or medium which will carry out this business a limited company under the Companies Act. I press this point upon the Government as a matter of convenience for everybody concerned. I rather deprecate the continuing creation of more and more exotic bodies. There have to be special Clauses put into Bills to say that their accounts shall be conducted according to the best commercial standards, and that they must make a report of this, that and the other. 334 They will have statutory duties to make reports, with standard forms relating to quorums, duties, obligations and responsibilities of directors, and so on.
