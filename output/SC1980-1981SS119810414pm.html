Education (Scotland) (No. 2) Bill
New Clause 1
AMENDMENT OF EDUCATION (SCOTLAND) ACT 1980

(1)After subsection (3) of section 1 of the Education (Scotland) Act 1980, there shall be inserted the following subsection:—

"(3A) It shall be the duty of all education authorities in Gaelic-speaking areas to provide education in Gaelic".

  1. (2)At the end of subsection (5) of the said section (1) there shall be inserted:—
  2. "'(d) Gaelic-speaking areas' means—
    1. the Highland Region;
    2. The Western Isles Island Council;
    3. Argyllshire and the Inner Hebrides; and
    4. Perthshire".'
