Third Standing Committee on Delegated Legislation
Tuesday 9 July 1996

[MR. DONALD ANDERSON in the Chair]

Draft Parliamentary Constituencies (England) (Miscellaneous Changes) Order 1996

10.30 am

The Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State for the Home Department (Mr. Tom Sackville)
: I beg to move, 4

That the Committee has considered the draft Parliamentary Constituencies (England) (Miscellaneous Changes) Order 1996.

The Boundary Commission's proposals are eminently sensible, and I commend them to the Committee.

Question put and agreed to.

Resolved,

That the Committee has considered the draft Parliamentary Constituencies (England) (Miscellaneous Changes) Order 1996.

Committee rose at half-past Ten o'clock.
