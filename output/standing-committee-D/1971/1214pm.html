The Chairman
: To take those points seriatim as far as the quorum is concerned, it is laid down in Standing Orders. I have to operate the Standing Orders and, therefore, nothing can be done about it. I can allow a considerable time to elapse before the locks are actually locked. I make it a practice to consult at least one member of the Committee to ensure that the time is appropriate to announce the locking of doors. I try to get it through as quickly as I can, but if a general wish is represented to me through the proper channels that longer time should be given before ordering the locking of doors I shall attend to it.
