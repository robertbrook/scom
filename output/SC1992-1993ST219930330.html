Second Standing Committee on Statutory Instruments, &c.
Tuesday 30 March 1993

[Mr. FRANK COOK in the Chair]

Draft Child Support Appeals (Jurisdiction of Courts) Order 1993

10.30 am

The Parliamentary Secretary to the Lord Chancellor's Department (Mr. John M. Taylor)
: I beg to move,

That the Committee has considered the draft Child Support Appeals (Jurisdiction of Courts) Order 1993.

This is a measure to provide the correct and appropriate forum for disputes of parentage in the realm of child support appeals.

Question put and agreed to.

Committee rose at twenty-nine minutes to Eleven o'clock.
