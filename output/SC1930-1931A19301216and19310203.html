STANDING COMMITTEE A.
Tuesday, 16th December, 1930.
RABBITS BILL.
[OFFICIAL REPORT.]
[Lieut.-Colonel Sir A. LAMBERT WARD (in the absence of Sir SAMUEL ROBERTS) in the Chair.]

The CHAIRMAN
: There being no Quorum present, and as it does not appear to be the wish of the Committee to meet again before the Recess, the Committee stands adjourned until Tuesday, 27th January.

Mr. HANBURY
: May we make it a week later?

The CHAIRMAN
: Certainly.

Committee adjourned accordingly at Twenty Minutes after Eleven o'Clock until Tuesday, 3rd February.
