Tuesday 1 February 1994

[MR. MICHAEL J. MARTIN in the Chair]

Local Government etc. (Scotland) Bill

10.30 am

The Chairman
: I cannot proceed because the hon. Member for Banff and Buchan (Mr. Salmond), who is not a member of the Committee, is present in the body of the Committee. I ask him to leave before we start our proceedings.

Mr. Andrew Welsh
(Angus, East)
: On a point of order, Mr. Martin.

The Chairman
: It is not a point of order. I am the one who is raising a point of order. Will the hon. Member for Banff and Buchan withdraw? [ Interruption.] I must ask the hon. Gentleman to withdraw. The Committee cannot commence, so I shall suspend proceedings for 15 minutes.

10.31 am

Sitting suspended.

10.46 am

On resuming—

The Chairman
: I see that the the hon. Member for Banff and Buchan is still present. I ask him, for the good order of the Committee, to leave. [ Interruption.] In that case, I call Mr. Kirkhope.

Motion made, and Question put,

That the Chairman do report Mr. Alex Salmond to the House.—[ Mr. Kirkhope.]

4

Mr. Welsh
: On a point of order, Mr. Martin. Can you explain the reason for that decision? Is it the lack of qualification of hon. Members representing English constituencies—

The Chairman
: Order. A motion is before the Committee and I have put the Question. As the hon. Gentleman knows, I do not have to explain that.

Question put:

The Committee divided: Ayes 12, Noes 1.
