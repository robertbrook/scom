The estimates before us today represent above all the Government's continuing commitment to the national health service in Scotland. They reflect the Conservative party's clear manifesto commitment that health expenditure would rise year on year in real terms. The figures will be well known to all members of the Committee. This year, spending on health in Scotland will be almost £4.2 billion. That is an increase in money terms of 5.3 per cent. on last year, and of 1.3 per cent. after movements in pay and prices have been taken into account.
