Excluding the UK continental shelf, our share of total GDP has increased steadily. It is generally accepted that estimates of GDP per head are the best indicator of trends in relative prosperity. Scottish performance in that respect has been most positive. Recent figures show an increase in Scottish GDP per head as a percentage of the UK figure in every year since 1989. Scotland's GDP performance during the recession has been very strong.
