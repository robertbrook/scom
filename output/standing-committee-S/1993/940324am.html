Mr. George Robertson (Hamilton): On a point of order, Mr. Martin. We are about to discuss clause 61, which is the first clause in part II relating to water and sewerage.

I know that that is not a matter for you, Mr. Martin, but I suggest that it would be premature for the Committee to consider this part of the Bill. The Government will want time to reconsider their views on the Bill in the light of the referendum result and statements made by Conservative councillors at the Convention of Scottish Local Authorities conference at Dunblane yesterday.

I seek your advice, Mr. Martin, about whether you would be willing to accept the following motion:
