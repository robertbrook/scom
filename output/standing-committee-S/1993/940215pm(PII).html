That would be serious, because its representation, in terms of services, and its accountability to its own local community would risk being seriously diluted. Travel-to-work areas, shopping patterns and other, anecdotal, evidence show a clear pattern that suggests that the people of north-east Fife make much greater reference to Dundee and Perth than they do to Kirkcaldy and Glenrothes.
