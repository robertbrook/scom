debates
title Draft Disclosure of Interests in Shares (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 1993

4.30 pm

Motion, made, and Question proposed,

That the Committee has considered the draft Disclosure of Interests in Shares (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 1993.—[Mr. Neil Hamilton.]

Mr. Stuart Bell (Middlesbrough): The House of Commons debated this matter some months ago. I have nothing to add to the Minister's statement.

Col. 4

Mr. Anthony Steen (South Hams): I shall make a short intervention. It seems that whatever we say in this Committee does not matter because, as I understand it, we cannot affect or change the statutory instrument in any way. If that is so, there seems no point in making a speech.

The Parliamentary Under-Secretary of State for Corporate Affairs (Mr. Neil Hamilton): I applaud my hon. Friend's deregulated behaviour and I confirm his understanding of the constitutional position.

Question put and agreed to.

Resolved,

That the Committee has considered the draft Disclosure of Interests in Shares (Amendment) (No. 2) Regulations 1993

Committee rose at twenty-nine minutes to Five o'clock.
