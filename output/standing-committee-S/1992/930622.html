Motion made, and Question proposed,

Col. 4

Ms. Joan Walley (Stoke-on-Trent, North): In view of the formal moving of the order by the Minister for Transport in London, who has responsibility for such matters, there is nothing that I want to add to prolong the proceedings.

Question put and agreed to.

Resolved,

That the Committee has considered the draft Department of Transport (Fees) (Amendment) Order 1993.

Committee rose at twenty-nine minutes to Eleven o'clock.
