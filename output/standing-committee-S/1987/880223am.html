The honourable Gentleman's first question asked about the effect of new section 23A in the Rent (Scotland) Act 1984. Because the new section begins:

"Nothing in this Part of this Act applies to a tenancy or right of occupancy if

the conditions set out apply, it disapplies sections 23 and 24 of the 1984 Act from excluded tenancies, as defined by new section 23A.

The references to rateable values in part VII of the 1984 Act either are left alone, if they refer to rateable value at some specified date, or are repealed entirely by schedule 10 to the Bill. However, that will not take effect until 1 April 1989, when the abolition of Domestic Rates (Scotland) Act 1987 comes into effect.
