Clause 1
SCOTTISH HOMES

Amendment proposed [this day]: No. 48, in page 2, line 10, at end insert

No. 57, in page 2, line 10, at end insert—

  • '(fa) assessing the Housing needs of each area of Scotland and providing and assisting the provision of housing to meet these needs;
  • (fb) providing and assisting the provision of special needs housing;
  • (fc) providing and assisting the provision of housing for the homelessness;
