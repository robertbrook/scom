Let me say a little about our thinking on exemptions from tenants' choice. Basically, we see tenants' choice and the right to buy as alternative ways in which the public sector tenant can choose to change his current arrangements.

We believe that, so far as possible, the right to buy and tenants' choice should apply to the same tenants.
