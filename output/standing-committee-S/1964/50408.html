Incentives, of course, are available for new enterprises, as the location of the Lochaber pulp mill shows. But the Local Employment Act is not enough for a remote area. In moving the first Amendment to the Bill I said that I was concerned that any future Secretary of State might say that the only proper way in which the Highlands would be able to play a more effective part in the economic and social development of the nation would be through becoming some sort of nature reserve for the relaxation of the workers in the existing connurbations.
