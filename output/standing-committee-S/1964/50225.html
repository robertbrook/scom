I put it to him, however, that if his point of view were adopted a Schedule to a Bill would be very rare. He would almost always seek to bring into a Clause the detailed provisions which are normally contained in Schedules. Although it is quite legitimate to put in a Schedule matters of a relatively detailed or procedural kind, if we were to bring 154 them into Clauses we would have excessively long and complicated Clauses.
