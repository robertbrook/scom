Clause 1.—(ESTABLISHMENT OF HIGHLAND DEVELOPMENT BOARD.)

Further Amendment proposed [25th March], In page 1, line 25, leave out subsection (4) and insert:

(4) The Board shall consist of not more than seven members elected by the local government electors for the said area in accordance with rules to be made by the Secretary of State and in making such rules the Secretary of State shall have regard to the population, the distribution and pursuits of the population, the geographical character and the local government boundaries of the several parts of the area, and such other circumstances which appear to him to affect the matter.


(5) Such rules shall be made by statutory instrument which shall be of no effect unless it is approved by a resolution of each House of Parliament, and the power to make such rules includes power to vary or revoke them by a subsequent statutory instrument.— [Mr. Hendry.]
