Traffic Areas (Reorganisation) Order 1991

4.30 pm

The Minister for Roads and Traffic (Mr. Christopher Chope)
: I beg to move,

That the Committee has considered the Traffic Areas (Reorganisation) Order 1991.

4 The object of the order is to amend the 1990 order to include Cumbria in the definition of the revised northwestern traffic area.

Question put and agreed to.

Resolved.
