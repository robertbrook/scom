The amendments relate to another aspect of the 75 per cent. test. In calculating the total premiums payable for purposes of the test, a proportion can be disregarded where premiums are payable more often than once it year because such premiums often contain a loading charge to recoup the higher administration costs of dealing with monthly or weekly payments.
