Draft Local Authorities (Charges for Overseas Assistance and Public Path Orders) Regulations 1996

4.30 pm

Motion made, and Question proposed,

That the Committee has considered the draft Local Authorities (Charges for Overseas Assistances and Public Path Orders) Regulations 1996. [Sir Paul Beresford.]

Mr Keith Vaz
(Leicester, East)
: The Opposition do not oppose the regulations.

Question put and agreed to.

Resolved,

That the Committee has considered the draft Local Authorities (Charges for Overseas Assistance and Public Path Orders) Regulations 1996.

Committee rose at half-past Four o'clock.
