I want also to thank the Official Report, police and attendants, and the civil servants who keep the promoter and the Minister in line. I have thoroughly enjoyed our proceedings. I am glad that it is not going on for another Wednesday. Other Bills are nudging behind. I thank hon. Members for their co-operation.
